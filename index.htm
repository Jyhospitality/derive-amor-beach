    About Amor Group of Hotels

    Welcome to Amor Group of Hotels

    The Spanish word "amor" (which means "love") is something that permeates all we do. The Amor Group of Hotels is a brand of boutique hotels that was lovingly constructed and offers opulent experiences at affordable prices. When you stay with us when travelling with loved ones, you will have a comfortable home-away-from-home experience since we uphold the "Atithi Devo Bhava" ideal.

    We take great pride in providing quick and considerate services. The wellbeing of our visitors is our top priority, and everyone on the Amor team strives to make your and your loved ones' stay enjoyable and hassle-free.

    luxury at picturesque locations

    All our properties are prominently located along the beach coast of Goa. Various tourist attractions and places of interest are located with a range of 15kms.
