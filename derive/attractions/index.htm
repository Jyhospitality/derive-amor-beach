    Chapora fort

    AttractionMonument
    Distance12km

    Popularly known as the 'Dil Chahta Hai fort', this ancient laterite fort is located on the Chapora river. Built by the Portuguese in 1717, the fort has a long and fascinating history attached to it. A perfect serene escape, the tourist spot offers beautiful views of the sea and Chapora River. Visit this ancient marvel located 30 Kms from our hotel near Candolim Beach.

    Reis Magos Fort

    AttractionAdventure
    Distance7.5 km

    Standing tall and proud overseeing and protecting the taluka of Bardez, across the river from the capital city of Panaji, this fort was one of the first bastions of the Portuguese rulers against enemy invasion. The structure exudes majesty and grandeur and though it is not the biggest fort in Goa, it still commands respect and awe. Pre-dating even the prestigious and much larger Fort Aguada, and situated at the narrowest point of the mouth of the Mandovi, it’s skilful construction and strategic placement made this fort nearly impregnable and it was an important asset to the Portuguese colonists.

    Snow park Baga

    AttractionAmusement
    Distance5.5 km

    Play a game of volleyball with friends & family over a field of snow. Located in Baga, this icy cold amusement Snow Park offers plenty of snow-activities for adults and kid. Unwind and enjoy a drink in an ice glass at the ice bar which happens to be Goa's first ice bar. The snow park is 4 kms away from Derive Hotel by Amor.

    Vagator Beach

    AttractionBeach
    Distance9 km

    This most photographed beach of Goa forms a bay that curves from the headland to the hillock crowned by the Chapora Fort. This beautiful arc of sand is located about 22 kms from Panaji and is part of the 30 km stretch of beach coastline along the west coast of Goa. Adjoining Anjuna, Vagator is secluded, crescent shaped and situated on the Caisua bay along the Chapora river basin in the shadow of Chapora Fort. During the tourist season, it is a favorite venue for midnight parties. There are a number of buses that run from Mapusa and Calangute beach to Vagator. The nearest interstate bus station is at Mapusa.

    Anjuna Beach

    AttractionBeach
    Distance10.2 km

    Anjuna was made famous by the ‘flower power and peace’ generation of the sixties and early seventies. And later by the ‘trance’ parties. Located about 18 kms from Panaji, the beach is known for its breeze-catching palms, soft sand, and the unusual rocky formation overlying a cove of whitish sand. and black rock that juts into the sea. It is now famous for its weekly Flea Market, which draws legions of visitors every Wednesday and bargains can be had on apparel, footwear, jewellery, footwear, chess sets—and yak cheese. The village of Anjuna is a five square mile enclosure nestling between the Arabian Sea and the Hill overlooking the beach.

    Candolim Beach

    AttractionBeach
    Distance1.9 km

    Take a long pleasant & relaxing walk on the Candolim beach as the waves splashes on you. Candolim Beach is one the famous attractions in North Goa and the beach coastline stretches for a length of 30 km that is from the Aguada Fort up to the Chapora beach. The beach is just 1 km from our hotels near Candolim beach.

    Baga beach

    AttractionBeach
    Distance4.3 km

    An extension of Calangute beach, the Baga beach is a long sandy beach lined up with shacks & fishing boats. The exciting nightlife & flea market offering gypsy stuff and eateries attract many tourists to this spot. Spend some memorable time with your family in the lap of nature located 3.5 km away from Derive Hotel by Amor, Candolim.

    Fort Aguada

    AttractionMonument
    Distance6.1 km

    This seventeenth-century Portuguese fort is definitely there in the itinerary of Goan tourists. The Aguada fort is epitome of Portuguese architecture built in the 17th century. One can enjoy the breath-taking views of Mandovi River and the Arabian Sea from the fort. The Aguada fort is 6 kms from Derive Hotel in Candolim.

    Calangute Beach

    AttractionBeach
    Distance3.1 km

    Known as the Queen of beaches in Goa, Calangute beach is the longest beach in North Goa. Visited by thousands of domestic and international tourists, the beach is one of the busiest tourist spots in Goa. Water sports and other adventure activities are available here for the enjoyment of tourists. The beach is 2 km from your stay at Derive Hotel.
