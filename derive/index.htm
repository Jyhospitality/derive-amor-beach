    Hotel Derive by Amor

    Indulge in a luxurious escape

    Embrace the charm of breathtaking views of the Arabian sea from Hotel Derive by Amor.
    Located near the popular Candolim beach, Hotel Derive is a boutique budget hotel and a paradise for travelers & backpackers who are seeking the trio -sun, sea, and sand- for their holidays. One can connect with the vibrant culture and nearby interesting places of this amazing holiday destination, or can just relax with their toes in the beach sand. Explore Candolim’s friendly markets, beach shacks, cafes, nightlife, and more from our chic, contemporary hotel offering quality accommodation.
