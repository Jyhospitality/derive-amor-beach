Candolim

    Candolim is located just beside the Calangute village and is home to Goa’s popular resorts, hotels, pubs, restaurants and other attractions.
    Unlike the busiest Calangute, Candolim is comparatively a bit secluded.
    The Candolim beach here is popular to experience sunbathing and to take occasional dips in the cool Arabian seawater.
    The area here is also nice for people who are interested in shopping and dining close to beachfront.
    The nightlife at Candolim attracts locals and tourists alike with famous and popular discotheque and pubs like Sinq. It is one of the most sought after beaches in North Goa when it comes to experiencing water sports like parasailing, snorkelling, boat ride, jet skiing and scuba diving in the Arabian sea.

    Close to the beach is the famous Aguada Fort, a well-preserved seventeenth-century Portuguese fort. Standing on the on Sinquerim Beach long with a lighthouse, this fort is one of the most visited attractions in Goa.
    Offering mesmerizing views of the surrounding areas, Fort Aguada is an epitome of Portuguese heritage and architecture.
    Candolim is about 12.5 km from Panaji and can be reached by taking a car or bike which takes around 35-40 minutes to reach. One can also take a bus service to reach this sandy beach.
